The Beginning of Christie and Chris's Cooking Cabin

The creation of a legacy

What started in 1993 has now turned into a famously known cooking company that begin in the Yellowstone, Wyoming. You guessed it, in a cabin. Christie and Chris's love of cooking took them to knew levels of excitement of the transformation of food. What started back then turned into a cooking book of their favorite meals in 2000 and now a youtube show that started in 2019. From the beginning to now you can see Christie and Chris partake in adventrues to explore the food around them. They have had many opportunities to share their experiences with others and their community. Their goal from when they first started is to inspire the next generation to enjoy cooking.

Exclusive Recipe From Their Original CookBooks

Recipe is from 1995

List of Ingredients Needed
  • 3 lbs of Chicken
  • 1 tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp of salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic pepper, and onion powder
Preparation
  1. Start by preparing 3 chicken breasts and cutting them in half
  2. Cut into cubes and rub olive oil with salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder
  3. Once coated place on frying pan and cook until golden brown on outside
  4. After cooked to golden brown, turn burner to low and let cook all the way through
  5. Serve with salad and fruit for an easy and healthy meal
